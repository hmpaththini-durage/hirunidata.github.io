Toronto Retail Analytics (SQL Server)

Excited to share my new SQL Analytics Project – Toronto Retail! I worked on building a retail analytics project in SQL Server, focusing on real-world business use cases.

Key Analyses:

  • Customer Insights – Number of orders per customer & total revenue by customer
  • Revenue Analysis – Monthly revenue trends & Year-over-Year (YoY) growth using LAG()
  • Product Performance – Top products by revenue and units sold in the last 90 days
  • Marketing Impact – Campaign lift analysis to evaluate ROI
  • Customer Segmentation – RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) scoring model

Skills Strengthened:

  • SQL Server (CTEs, window functions, aggregations)
  • Data-driven storytelling through queries
  • Translating business questions into analytical solutions
